The Friends of Springwood Lake are open to volunteers and receiving updated information which can be helpful to restoring and maintaining the lake, as well as minimizing crime in and around the Springwood Lake Neighborhood Area. We would be delighted to hear from you if you're interested in getting involved or partnering with us.

The Friends of Springwood Lake are all 100% volunteer and not compensated for our work. Our goal is to improve and enhance the vitality of Springwood Lake. Please check our Documents Repository. If you have any further questions you can send them to us for possible response as time allows. Thank you for your understanding, interest and especially your Support!

friendsofspringwoodlake@gmail.com

As more information is researched and becomes available, Friends of Springwood Lake will update it accordingly. If you have any corrections please feel free to send them to us.
