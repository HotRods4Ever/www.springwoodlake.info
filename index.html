Welcome!

The goal of this web site is for friends, residents and neighbors of Springwood Lake, as well as its surrounding community, to be able to share relevant documents related to the lake, the lake HOA and the potential future lake restoration efforts being investigated and worked on by various Richland County and other State Agencies.
Information presented on SpringwoodLake.info has been gathered via a variety of sources. Please send an e-mail to the Friends of Springwood Lake if you have any corrections or additional documentation you would like to provide and share with your neighbors.
