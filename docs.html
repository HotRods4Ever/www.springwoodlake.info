Documents Repository

PLEASE CONTACT US TO UPDATE, CORRECT OR EXPAND UPON THE INFO ON THIS PAGE.

This page is intended to serve as a document repository for information related to Springwood Lakeside Individual Property Owner's Association. Information was gathered via multiple sources, including but not limited to, Registrar of Deed's Office, State and County agencies, Neighbors and responses from HOA leadership.
This page will also link to, and provide information about the requirements for HOAs in South Carolina and Richland County.

As more information is researched and becomes available, the Friends of Springwood Lake will update it.
